About me

My name is Peter Marosi, I am 21 years old. I was born in Slovakia, in the city of Komarno. I'm a resident of Kolarovo. I am a student of the Faculty of Electronics and Informatics at the Slovak University of Technology at Bratislava. I am currently in the third year of my study and have classes 3 days a week. I am interested in programming, sports, computers and video games. I am also interested in the field of artificial intelligence, web development and game development.The hobby that takes up the biggest part of my life is fitness. I have been training or taking part in some kind of sport for almost 10 years. I started with wrestling for about 3 years and won several awards; then I switched to bodybuilding. Altogether I have been training naturally for 5 years. I haven't competed yet, but I am planning to do so in the future, maybe after I finish my studies. Keeping up my training is very challenging at the moment because of collage, but I am trying to do my best.

My hobbies are:
  • Programming
  • Cycling
  • Reading
  • Playing video games
  • Computers
  • Health and fitness
